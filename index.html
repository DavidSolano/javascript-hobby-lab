Introduction

My hobby is rather simple, you can just play games. You can play whatever genre you want and whatever amount of games you want. There's RPG's and its related genres. Shooters and whatnot. Really, if you can think of it its probably a game or there is one similar to it.

Resources

  1. Twitch is our first resource for finding things related to my hobby. In there you can find a myriad of people playing games. Games you might never had heard of.
  2. Youtube is basically another twitch except this time you look up your game and again many people will have made videos about it. Reviews, tutorials and whatnot. All that jazz.
  3. Steam is a store for pc games. Steam has store wide sales every 3 months. Basically every season or major holiday. Although not going to lie the sales lately haven't been great, but hey that's just my opinion.

Did you know?

It is quite easy to sink in hundreds upon hundreds of hours into video games. That's just a fact of life right there. No going around it. At some point you'll find a game you sink hours into. Maybe not like in a row, but over time those hours build up.
